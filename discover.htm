FACTS ABOUT OSLOB, CEBU PROVINCE

  • Oslob is situated in the province of Cebu, in the Central Visayas region of the Philippines.
  • Coordinates: Approximately 9.5200° N latitude and 123.4333° E longitude.
  • The municipality covers an area of about 134.75 square kilometers.
  • Topography: Oslob features a mix of coastal areas, hills, and mountains, with the Tañon Strait to the east.
  • Demographics: Oslob has a population around 29,264.
  • Language: The primary language spoken is Cebuano, but Filipino and English are also understood.
  • Main Industries: Tourism, fishing, and agriculture are the primary economic activities.
  • Whale Sharks: Oslob is known for its whale shark (Butanding) watching activities, which attract local and foreign tourists annually.
  • Transportation: Oslob is accessible by bus or private vehicle from Cebu City, which is about 120 km away (3–4 hours).
  • Port: There is a small port in Oslob that connects to nearby islands.
  • Climate: Oslob experiences a tropical climate with distinct wet (June–December) and dry (January–May) seasons.
